Finding suitable PG accommodations can be a tedious process for students and working professionals, as it involves visiting multiple locations and gathering reliable information. Similarly, PG owners face challenges in reaching potential tenants and managing their property listings effectively.Currently, there is no centralized platform that provides an easy and transparent way to search for PGs, compare amenities, and communicate with owners.

The Find My PG Accommodation platform aims to streamline the process of finding and listing PG accommodations by providing a user-friendly web application. It allows users to easily search for PGs based on various criteria and offers dedicated dashboards for administrators, owners, and users to manage their respective activities effectively.

The Find My PG Accommodation platform offers a centralized and efficient solution for both PG seekers and owners. This project aims to create a reliable, transparent, and easy-to-use system that benefits the entire community involved in PG accommodations.
